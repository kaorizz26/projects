Chicken Fillet "Ala King"

Ala King:

Ala King is a delicious chicken dish with creamy mushroom sauce served over rice. It's a Filipino-inspired dish popular in McDonald's Philippines.

Fun Fact:

The Ala King meal was first introduced in McDonald's Philippines in 1980 and has since become a favorite comfort food for many customers.

If you want to cook Ala King, a delicious chicken dish with creamy mushroom sauce served over rice, here are the ingredients and steps to follow:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast or thigh fillets, sliced
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Mushroom soup
  • Milk
  • Oil for sautéing
  • Cooked rice

Cooking Steps:

  1. Heat a pan and add oil.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour mushroom soup and milk into the pan.
  5. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve the Ala King over a bed of rice.
