Shake Shake Fries

Fries:

Shake Shake Fries are seasoned french fries that come with a packet of seasoning that you can sprinkle and shake to add flavor. They are a popular side dish at McDonald's.

Fun Fact:

Shake Shake Fries were first introduced in 2005 in McDonald's Malaysia and quickly gained popularity, becoming available in various flavors like BBQ, Cheese, and Spicy.

If you want to make Shake Shake Fries, seasoned french fries that come with a packet of seasoning, here are the ingredients and steps to follow:

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • Seasoning packet (e.g., BBQ, Cheese, Spicy)

Cooking Steps:

  1. Preheat oil in a deep fryer or large pot.
  2. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  3. Fry the potatoes until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain excess oil and transfer the fries to a paper towel-lined plate.
  5. Open the seasoning packet and sprinkle it over the fries.
  6. Cover the fries and shake them gently to distribute the seasoning evenly.
  7. Serve the Shake Shake Fries while hot.
